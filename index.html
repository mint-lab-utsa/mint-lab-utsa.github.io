About the Lab

The MINT Lab at UTSA focuses on advanced polymer composites, additive manufacturing, rheology, and data-driven materials optimization. Our research integrates experimental characterization, computational modeling, and machine learning to design high-performance engineering materials.

Research Areas

Polymer Composites

Design and characterization of high-performance composite systems.

Additive Manufacturing

Process optimization and hybrid manufacturing strategies.

Rheology & Flow

Understanding melt behavior and solids loading transitions.

AI for Materials

Machine learning-driven materials design and optimization.
